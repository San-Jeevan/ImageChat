SackMoyesNow.com



This site was made for fun, but soon turned out to be not funny at all as United regressed from being a title winning side to top 4 challenger.
We, ironically, don't want Moyes to be sacked right now, but believe he should get the boot if the following patterns continues:

  • Negative lineup and tactics with the intention of avoiding defeat instead of going out to win the match
  • The usual suspects (Young, Welbeck, Giggs, Valencia etc.) getting picked match after match despite horrendous performances
  • Horrible interviews belittling the team quality and players
  • Failure to acknowledge the expectation of United'. We don't aim for top 4 , we aim for the title. We dont play to draw against big sides, we go out to win. We don't defend 1-goal leads, we get a 2-goal lead to create a buffer incase the opponents flukes a header in the dying minutes
  • Apply a pass and move style, or better apply your own style just dont hoof it upto Fellaini or whoever tall player is up front.

Still, he might come good, but time is certainly running out. And the pressure will rightfully build up...
